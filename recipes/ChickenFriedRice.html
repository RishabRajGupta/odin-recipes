Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This recipe for chicken fried rice is a delicious and satisfying dish that is perfect for a quick and easy weeknight meal. The rice is stir-fried with chicken, eggs, peas, carrots, and green onions, and seasoned with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil. The combination of flavors and textures is sure to impress your family and leave them wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the eggs and scramble until cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Add the chicken to the skillet and cook until heated through.
  4. Add the peas, carrots, and green onions to the skillet and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Add the rice, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sesame oil to the skillet and toss to combine.
  6. Return the eggs to the skillet and toss to combine.
  7. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until heated through.

Enjoy!
